CABRES BUILDERS

INCORPORATED (Manila)

CABRES Builders, Inc.

(formerly known as CMMC Construction, Inc.) is a young and dynamic company involved in construction, engineering, technical maintenance services, design and project management, surveying, interior design, fabrication and landscaping.

We started our commercial operation in 1986 (under the name of CMC Engineering) doing residential houses, low rise commercial buildings, minor horizontal construction and interior design and construction.

After 30 years, the company is now capable of providing technical expertise in construction, planning, designing, structural analysis, electrical and electronics communication engineering, construction management, interior design, landscaping and fabrication.

The company has done a number of low, mid-rise, and regular residential buildings, townhouse clusters, minor road construction, warehouses, industrial plant, schools, and offices.

Interior design and construction expertise were applied in a number of condominiums, residential, industrial and office projects. Aside from locally available interior materials and supplies, the company is capable of bringing in and utilizing imported raw materials known in the interior design and supply industry.

The company is also involved in land surveying, subdivision planning and development. Landscaping is one expertise that has also been applied to a number of projects.

Mission Statement

Our primary role as a local engineering and construction company is to design and build infrastructure facilities using locally available but advanced technology and people in architecture, engineering, electronics and interior design; needed to improve the quality of life of our fellowmen. In fulfilling this role, we firmly resolve to;

Strive for excellence in all our undertakings and faithfully adhere to the highest ethical and technical standards of our profession;

  • Provide quality, reliable, prompt and efficient service to our client to the best of our capabilities.
  • Treat our fellow workers with fairness and dignity-recognizing and rewarding their commitment and hard work and affording them the chance to develop their professional and technical skills.
  • Conduct ourselves as young Christians professionals in every client we serve, ever mindful of our basic social obligations and responsibilities.

In making this commitment, we reaffirm our belief that by doing our job well and giving our best in everything we do as a professionals, we can give our humble contribution to the development and progress of our country.
